Rockfish are native to Puget Sound

What is a rockfish?

A rockfish can be from one out of many species of rockfish, or the family of fish known as Sebastes.

Lures for rockfish

Rockfish are vivacious eaters, they have poor eyesights and will generally attack anything they can see or smell. Lures and bait are great options for them with lures being preferred as many rockfish species have to be returned to the water alive, and using bait risks gut hooking these creatures which is almost a guaranteed death for them.
